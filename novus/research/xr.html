Extended Reality Product Prototypes
扩展现实产品原型
[ English / 中文 ] Several prototypes was designed and fabricated, for example, products series consisting of 2 to 8 depth camera array for monitor, security, and automation. The prototypes, rebuilding real-time 3D environment, have the functions of object and behavior recognition with a machine learning interface. 设计和制作了若干原型，例如其中一个产品系列是由2-8个深感摄像头组成的阵列，可用于监测、安保、自动化。这个原型能够实时重建3D场景，同时具备机器学习界面，可进行物体识别和行为识别。
